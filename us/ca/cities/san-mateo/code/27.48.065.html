27.48.065 PARCEL COVERAGE.

A minimum of 20 percent at-grade open space shall be provided on the parcel, except in the /R overlay district where 10 percent minimum at-grade open space is required. Open space shall contain landscaping and/or decorative outdoor landscape elements, water features, paved or decorated surfaces of rock, stone, brick or other similar material (excluding driveways, parking, loading, or storage areas), and sculptural elements.

(Ord. No. 1991-18 § 25; Ord. No. 1987-1 § 18; Ord. No. 1985-17 § 46.)
